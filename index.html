About the Platform

This web-based platform helps students record, manage, and showcase their extracurricular activities such as sports, cultural activities, competitions, and certifications. Faculty can review student submissions, and administrators can generate reports for university records and placements.

Key Features

  • Student Login & Dashboard
  • Add/Upload Achievement Certificates
  • Faculty Review & Approval System
  • Achievement History & Filtering
  • Downloadable Reports for Placement
